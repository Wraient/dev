Internet Of things
This project was a hard project to do. In this project, I used a ESP8266 and a Relay Board to communicate with the server. The server was a webpage which served a simple html page with 4 buttons showing the status of the relay switch and a button to turn it on or off. The delay was about 2-3 seconds. I used django to serve the webpage and send request to the ESP8266 then the ESP8266 fetched the request and turned on or off IO switches which then made the relay turn on or off, respectively. The ESP8266 fetched requests every 3 seconds, hence the delay. The power usage of the whole project was low and economically beneficial. Whole project took about 1 week to complete and am proud of the end result. I made a overview of how it works. Please do not judge by the production quality.
