Internet Of things
In this one-week project, I took on the challenge of making an ESP8266 and a Relay Board communicate through a Django-powered server. The server featured a straightforward webpage with buttons to display and control the relay switch. Despite a brief 2-3 seconds delay, the system operated smoothly, and seeing everything come together was a satisfying experience.

The Django server consistently sent messages to the ESP8266, which dutifully executed tasks every 3 seconds. It manipulated switches to control the relay, bringing the entire setup to life. To ensure cost-effectiveness, I made a conscious effort to optimize power usage in the project.
